Under the hood

How our AI builds your plate

We combine data from your profile with our food graph—ingredients, nutrients, recipes, and restaurant items—to recommend meals that feel hand-picked for you.

Smart matching

Every recipe or meal option is scored on multiple axes: macro fit, ingredient match, cooking time, complexity, and how closely it aligns with what you’ve liked before.

When you save, cook, or skip a suggestion, the system learns. Over time, your feed shifts toward the meals, snacks, and drinks you actually follow through on.
